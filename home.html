Additionaly he is the one who gaved the city it's orignial name Mount Salus, means "mountain of health". Which was by his plantation home of the same name. Notably the name changed to Clinton in 1828.

It ushered in Jim Crow and segregation laws that took the rights that newly freed African American slaves gained during the Reconstruciton era.
